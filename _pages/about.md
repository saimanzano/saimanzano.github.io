Hello! 
My name is Saioa Manzano-Morales. I am a PhD student at the [Comparative Genomics Lab](http://cgenomics.org/) led by [Toni Gabaldón](https://scholar.google.es/citations?user=f5qDizoAAAAJ&hl=es&oi=ao) at the [Barcelona Supercomputing Center](https://www.bsc.es/) (BSC-CNS).

I am very passionate about what computational analyses can offer to the field of evolution. In particular, I am interested in the field of comparative genomics, and employing the unprecedented volume of genomic data from previously-unknown lineages to unveil one of the greatest mysteries of biology: the origin of eukaryotes. Apart from that, I am also curious about pan- and metagenomics.

Please do not hesitate to contact me!
